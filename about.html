Hi! I am currently a Phd candidate in political science at Stanford. My dissertation research examines political rhetoric about China in the United States, how it has evolved over time, and what drives anti-China speech and attitudes. Since I enjoy playing with all types of data and creating data visualizations , I created this site to share some of what I have done, related to my research and otherwise.

I have also worked as a data scientist at Nielsen, working on audience measurment on mobile. In the past, I worked in China as an academic researcher, an editor for the China Elections and Governance website, and an English instructor. I still have a deep appreciation for legit Sichuan food and da pan ji.
